FTC Disclosure Requirements

As an affiliate marketer, you are legally required to disclose your affiliate relationship with Bluestone. Here's how to stay compliant:

Where to Include Disclosures

  • Blog Posts: Add disclosure at the top of the article, before the first affiliate link
  • Email Marketing: Include disclosure near the top of the email, not just in footer
  • Social Media: Use #ad, #affiliate, or "affiliate link" in your posts
  • Video Content: Verbal disclosure + written disclosure in description
  • Paid Ads: Clearly identify ads as sponsored or affiliate content

Compliant Disclosure Examples

Blog Post Disclosure:
"This post contains affiliate links. If you make a purchase through these links, I may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. I only recommend products I personally use and believe in."

Email Disclosure:
"This email contains affiliate links. I may earn a commission if you make a purchase."

Social Media Disclosure:
"#ad #affiliate - I earn a commission from purchases through my link"

‚ö†Ô∏è Legal Requirement
