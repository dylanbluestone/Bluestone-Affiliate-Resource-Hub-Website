FTC Disclosure Requirements

Where to Include Disclosures

  • Email Marketing: Include disclosure near the top of the email, not just in footer
  • Social Media: Use #ad, #affiliate, or "affiliate link" in your posts
  • Video Content: Verbal disclosure + written disclosure in description
